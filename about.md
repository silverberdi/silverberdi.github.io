## A Journey Through Systems

My path has taken me through Finance, Healthcare, and Telco—industries where "good enough" isn't an option. Whether I was founding and scaling a software factory managing 50+ projects or leading technical strategy for national-scale banking applications, the goal has always been the same: precision, scalability, and impact.

Currently, I serve as a Technical Consultant at Perficient, where I bridge the gap between complex backend microservices and modern, responsive frontends.

## Beyond the Stack

I've worn many hats: Solution Architect, Tech Lead, Scrum Master, and Product Owner. This mix of roles has taught me that the best technical solutions are worthless if they don't align with the product vision.

Today, my focus has shifted toward the next frontier: Generative AI integration. I'm obsessed with how we can move beyond simple chatbots to create autonomous, "AI-based" development pipelines that actually understand the *why* behind the code.

## Why This Blog?

I'm currently pursuing a dual MBA and Master in International Administration, while wrapping up my B.S. in Software Engineering. This blog is where my academic research, my decades of professional "battle scars," and my experiments with AI like Dairector meet.

I'm here to share what works, what fails, and how we can build a more intelligent future for software engineering.
