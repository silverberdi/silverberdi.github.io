This Privacy Policy describes how Silverio Bernal's professional blog ("the Site") handles information when you visit or interact with it.

## Site Owner

This Site is the personal professional blog of Silverio Bernal. It is used to share technical articles, professional opinions, and project updates.

## LinkedIn OAuth and API Usage

The Site and related automation tools may use LinkedIn OAuth and the LinkedIn API solely for the purpose of publishing approved professional content on behalf of Silverio Bernal. This usage is owner-controlled and limited to content that has been explicitly reviewed and authorized.

## Data Security

LinkedIn access tokens, secrets, and any authentication credentials are stored securely and are never exposed publicly. No LinkedIn data obtained through API access is sold, shared, or used for any purpose other than the authorized publication of content.

## Logging and Metadata

Like most websites, the Site and its hosting infrastructure may collect standard technical information, including:

- Request logs (IP addresses, timestamps, requested pages)
- Browser metadata (user agent, referrer headers)
- IP-derived technical logs
- Error logs for operational purposes

This data is used only for operational maintenance, security, and troubleshooting. It is not sold or shared with third parties.

The Site may contain links to external services and professional platforms, including but not limited to LinkedIn, GitHub, and other professional networks. This Privacy Policy applies only to this Site. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach Silverio Bernal through the public professional channels available on the Site.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
